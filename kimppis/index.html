Smarter way to share a taxi!

Smarter traffic

Kimppis is a smart service that connects taxi passengers going the same direction. It helps to buddy up and to shorten the taxi lines as more people share a ride. Kimppis is truly smarter traffic.

It's free!

Like best things in life, Kimppis is free. Yet, it saves tons of your money, time and nerves. And our mother nature loves Kimppis too.

Launch mobile app!

Build it!

When smart people get together, amazing things happen. That is how Kimppis happened. The service was done in just 48 hours in Aaltoes Build it event. You may find some goofy things as we didn't sleep too much that weekend. We hope you enjoy sharing taxis with Kimppis!
